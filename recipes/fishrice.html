Fish Rice Recipe

Description

Fish Rice recipe is flexible makeover from jollof, rice and stew, fried rice to other style of rice preparation. Well ganished and mixed with sea-fish (other kind of fish can be chosen).

A versatile fish rice recipe can serve as a makeover for styles such as Nigerian Jollof rice, fried rice, rice and stew, or Indian biryani. The dish is customizable with different fish like mackerel, kingfish, or croaker for frying, or salmon and snapper for steaming. The meal can be garnished with aromatics like curry leaves or crunchy additions like cashews and raisins.
