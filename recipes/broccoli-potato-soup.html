Broccoli Potato Soup Recipe

Description

This creamy broccoli soup is a healthy, comforting dish featuring a smooth potato and cashew base.

Ingredients

  • 1 large yellow onion and 1 large carrot.
  • 3 garlic cloves and 2 pounds Yukon gold potatoes (about 6 medium).
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil and 1 quart vegetable broth.
  • 1/2 cup raw unsalted cashews and 1 teaspoon kosher salt (divided).
  • 1 pound frozen broccoli florets (or 4 cups small steamed broccoli florets).
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme and 3/4 teaspoon dried dill.
  • 2 teaspoons white wine vinegar and 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard.

Steps

  1. Dice the onion and mince the garlic.
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut them into bite-sized chunks.
  3. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the onion and saute for 5 minutes, then add the garlic and saute for an additional minute.
  5. Add the vegetable broth, cashews, potatoes, and 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt; bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce to a rapid simmer and cook for about 15 minutes until the potatoes are tender and falling apart.
  7. Meanwhile, thaw frozen broccoli under hot water and chop into smaller florets as necessary.
  8. Place the broccoli in a bowl and mix it with 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt and black pepper to taste.
  9. Peel and grate the carrot into long strips.
  10. When the potatoes are tender, transfer the mixture to a blender using a ladle.
  11. Add the thyme, dill, white wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, and another 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt.
  12. Hold the lid tight and blend for a minute or two until fully creamy.
  13. Pour the creamy soup back into the pot, add the broccoli and carrot, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  14. Taste and add additional salt if necessary.

Notes

  • If you’re cooking fresh broccoli, here’s how to simulate frozen: Cut it into very small florets.
  • Then steam it but cook it a few minutes longer so that it’s very tender (not crisp tender as indicated in the recipe).
  • Leave out any seasoning, since you’ll add salt as part of the soup recipe.
