Pounded Yam with Egusi Soup Recipe

Description

Nigerian egusi soup, also known as efo elegusi, is a delicious and versatile soup served with pounded yam, which is a staple dish.

Ingredients

  • Pounded Yam: 1 medium yam, water, and salt.
  • Egusi Soup Base: 1 1/2 cups egusi, 1/4 cup palm oil, and 1 tablespoon fermented locust beans.
  • Proteins: 3 tablespoons ground crayfish, 1 cup dried fish, 3 smoked mackerel fillets, and 1 cup mixed seafood.

Steps

Pounded Yam

  1. Peel the yam, slice, and wash it.
  2. Cook with water and salt until completely soft.
  3. Using a mortar and pestle, pound the yam until soft and sticky, sprinkling with yam water at intervals.
  4. Mold into desired shapes and set aside.
